Introduction

Advtech's Bots ("we", "us", "our") operates the Selena Discord bot ("Selena"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Selena. By using Selena, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Discord User ID (UUID): We collect your unique Discord User ID to provide personalized services such as tracking your activity, leveling system, currency system, and other features.

Guild Data: We collect data specific to the guilds (servers) you are part of, including but not limited to server IDs, channel IDs, and message content for processing commands.

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision: To provide and maintain the functionality of Selena, including leveling, currency management, birthday tracking, and other features.

Communication: To send you notifications and updates related to your usage of Selena.

Analytics: To understand and analyze how you use Selena and to improve our services.

3. Data Retention

Retention Period: Your data is retained for more than 30 days. This includes your Discord UUID and associated activity data.

Data Deletion: You have the right to delete your data at any time. You can use the command `/delete_data` to remove all your data from our records.

4. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other types of misuse.

5. Your Rights

Access and Update: You have the right to access and update your personal information stored by us using the command `/request_data`.

Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data using the command `/delete_data`.

Opt-Out: You can opt-out of using Selena at any time by using the command `/optout`.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
